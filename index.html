VIT Reading Club

“Shape the horizon of knowledge at VIT”

Read Today, Lead Tomorrow

Mission

  • Promote reading habits among students through books, newspapers, and magazines.
  • Foster meaningful discussions, storytelling, and reviews that encourage critical thinking.
  • Build a sense of community that supports personal growth and lifelong learning.
  • Develop communication, creativity, and listening skills in a fun and relaxed environment.

Vision

  • Inspire students to explore diverse literature and broaden their perspectives.
  • Cultivate habits of self-expression, critical thinking, and intellectual curiosity.
  • Create a vibrant community of thoughtful and curious readers on campus.
  • Shape future leaders by nurturing creativity, leadership, and passion for literature.

Join us and be part of a community of readers!
